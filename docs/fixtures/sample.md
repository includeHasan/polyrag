# Remote Work Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible to apply for remote work arrangements. Part-time employees may be considered on a case-by-case basis at the discretion of their department head.

## Equipment

The company will provide the following equipment to remote workers:
- A laptop computer (MacBook Pro 14" or Dell XPS 13)
- An external monitor (27" 4K)
- A keyboard and mouse of choice (up to $150)
- A one-time home office stipend of $500

## Working Hours

Remote workers are expected to maintain their regular working hours (9:00 AM to 5:00 PM in their local time zone). Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM, during which employees should be available for meetings and synchronous communication.

## Communication

- Slack must be checked at least once every two hours during working hours
- All meetings should default to video-on
- A weekly check-in with the direct manager is required

## Security

Remote workers must:
- Use the company VPN when accessing internal systems
- Store work-related data only on company-provided devices
- Use a password manager for all work-related accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts

## Performance

Performance is evaluated based on outcomes, not hours worked. Managers are expected to focus on results delivered, not time spent online.

## Reimbursement

The company will reimburse up to $50/month for home internet expenses. Receipts must be submitted through the expenses system.
