# Time Off and Leave Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of PTO per year, accruing at 1.67 days per month. Unused PTO carries over up to a maximum of 10 days; the rest is forfeited on January 1st.

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is separate from PTO. Employees receive 10 sick days per year, available immediately on the first day of employment. Unused sick days do not carry over.

## Parental Leave

Birthing parents receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Non-birthing parents receive 12 weeks. Parental leave can be taken any time within the first 12 months of birth or adoption.

## Bereavement

- Spouse, child, or parent: 5 days paid
- Sibling, grandparent, or in-law: 3 days paid
- Extended family: 1 day paid

## Jury Duty and Civic Leave

Unlimited paid leave for jury duty, voting, and other civic obligations. Provide documentation at least 24 hours in advance.

## Sabbatical

After 5 years of continuous service, employees are eligible for a 4-week paid sabbatical. After 10 years, an 8-week sabbatical. Sabbaticals must be taken in one continuous block.
