# Equipment Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

## Laptop and Hardware

All engineering and product staff receive a company laptop. The default model is the MacBook Pro 14" (M3 Pro, 18GB RAM, 512GB SSD) for engineering and the Dell XPS 13 for product managers. Hardware refresh occurs every 3 years.

## Peripherals

- **Monitor**: 27" 4K display (Dell U2723QE or equivalent)
- **Keyboard**: Any keyboard up to $150 (mechanical preferred)
- **Mouse**: Any mouse up to $80 (MX Master series recommended)
- **Headset**: Noise-cancelling headset for remote meetings (up to $300)
- **Webcam**: 1080p or better external webcam (up to $150)

## Home Office Stipend

A one-time $500 home office stipend is provided for new hires. Eligible items include:
- Office chair
- Standing desk converter
- Monitor arm
- Cable management
- Lighting

## Security Requirements

- Full-disk encryption required on all company devices
- Endpoint protection (CrowdStrike) must be installed and active
- USB storage devices are blocked by default policy

## Replacement

Lost or damaged equipment is replaced within 5 business days for laptops and 10 business days for peripherals. Theft requires a police report.
